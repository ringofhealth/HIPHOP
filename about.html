Meet Our Host

  • Cailleah Scott-Grimes

Cailleah Scott-Grimes, She Got Game Host

A perpetual asker of big questions, Cailleah works on both sides of the camera, trying to shed a little light and humo(u)r on the human experience. Her interest in probing creative minds springs from living and working in Canada, the US, Europe and Japan. She is a graduate of the University of Toronto’s Visual Studies and East Asian Studies programs, a freelance videographer and a visual artist.

When not shooting, story-boarding or painting, Cailleah is dead-lifting at the gym, singing a cappella, cooking brussel sprouts with bacon, or curling up with Japanese comics. A life of good fortune has led to working with gifted artists, musicians, writers and performers, but what about the women who want art, music, story-telling and drama all in one—those with a passion for video games?

Join Cailleah in diving headlong into the social and professional world of the female game-lover and the inspirational, cutting-edge women behind the scenes.

Dr. S2(squared),The Tech Wizard
One dark night while sneaking around a castle in Transylvania, Cailleah came across the secret work station of the mysterious Dr. S2. Although no one has ever seen his face, his wizard genius is renowned across the land. For centuries he was trapped, deep in Dracula’s chambers, until he and Cailleah devised a plan of escape. In exchange for his release, Dr. S2 has agreed to help Cailleah on her search for women who are changing the world. He works day and night as the great tech mastermind behind this project. Rumour has it that his motherboard is so powerful, it sometimes has sway over Lady Fate herself...
